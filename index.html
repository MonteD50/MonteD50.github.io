About the App

Listen to recordings from different languages and guess which one you're hearing. Fun, educational, and perfect for language lovers!

Features

  • Test your ability to recognize languages from audio clips.
  • Choose from Easy, Medium, or Hard modes.
  • Earn coins and use hints when needed.
  • Explore languages from regions like Europe, Asia, and Africa.
